# ashleys-photobooth

A fun web-based photobooth that lets you take or upload 4 photos and create vintage or modern photostrips!

## Features

- **Vintage Mode**: Black and white photos with classic photobooth vibes
- **Modern Mode**: Full color photos with custom notes and frames
- **Take Photos**: Use your camera with a countdown timer
- **Upload Photos**: Select and crop your own photos
- **Customize**: Choose colors, add notes, and include dates
- **Download**: Save your photostrip as a JPG

## Try It Out

https://ashleyhua.github.io/ashleys-photobooth/

## How It Works

1. Pick Vintage or Modern style
2. Take 4 photos or upload 4 photos
3. Customize your colors and add a message
4. Download your photostrip!

## Tech Stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript
